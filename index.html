Design Engineering Society

DesSoc is the departmental society branched from the Dyson School of Design Engineering at Imperial College London. We aim to provide our members extracurricular and social events to broaden their experience whilst they study at Imperial.

We want to ensure that all freshmen students join the department in the best way possible, so throughout the year we will run ‘Family’ events, as part of the Mums and Dads initiative, to help integrate them into the DesEng family. Throughout the year we run a variety of events such as dinners, pub crawls, film nights, football, quiz nights, workshops, and professional talks from alumni and industry. We hope you have a fantastic year.
